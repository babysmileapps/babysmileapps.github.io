Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: February 22, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the My Budget Plan application collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our services.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following information to ensure the proper functioning of the application:

  • Purchase Data: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. No banking data is stored on our servers; it is processed securely by the App Store.
  • Camera Access: The application requests access to the Camera only to allow scanning of receipts or invoices. No images are collected without your explicit action.

2. Use of Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the Premium features of the application
  • Process your transactions via the App Store
  • Improve the user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We share anonymized technical data with:

  • Apple App Store Services: For application management and purchases
  • RevenueCat: For validation and tracking of your Premium subscription
  • Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics: For app performance and crash reporting

Platform-Specific Services

iOS: This application uses Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics for performance monitoring. No third-party advertising networks are used on iOS.

4. Data Retention

We retain your transaction information as long as necessary to provide you with access to Premium services. You can request the deletion of your data by contacting us at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
